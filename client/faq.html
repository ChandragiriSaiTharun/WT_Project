Crop & Farming

Our experts can help identify crop diseases. Submit a support ticket with clear photos of affected plants, describe symptoms, and mention your crop type and location. You can also call our expert helpline at +91-9876543210 for immediate guidance.

Planting times vary by crop type and region:
Kharif crops (Rice, Cotton, Sugarcane): June-July
Rabi crops (Wheat, Barley, Peas): October-December
Zaid crops (Fodder, Watermelon): March-April
Always consider local weather conditions and soil preparation.

Healthy soil should be dark colored, crumbly, have good drainage, and rich in organic matter. Get a soil test done to check pH levels (ideal: 6.0-7.5), nutrient content, and organic matter percentage. Contact your local agricultural extension office for soil testing services.

Market & Pricing

Login to your Kisaan Connect account and visit the Marketplace section. You can view real-time market prices, track price trends for your crops, and get alerts when prices reach your target levels. We update prices daily from major mandis across India.

Go to the Marketplace, click "Add Crop", fill in details like crop name, quantity, price, and location. Upload clear photos of your produce. Your listing will be visible to buyers immediately. Make sure to provide accurate contact information for buyers to reach you.

Crop prices depend on: supply and demand, weather conditions, government policies, international market trends, transportation costs, storage facilities, and seasonal variations. Stay updated with market news and government announcements that might affect your crop prices.
